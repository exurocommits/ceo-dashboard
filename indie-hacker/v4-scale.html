Scale Smart, Not Hard

The principles that separate sustainable growth from burnout.

🤖

Automate Everything

Customer onboarding. Support tickets. Social media. Billing. If it's repeatable, automate it. Your time is for strategy.

📊

Metrics That Matter

MRR. Churn. LTV. CAC. Ignore vanity metrics. Focus on what drives decisions. Data-driven growth, not guesswork.

🎯

Niche Domination

Don't compete broadly. Own a niche. Expand from strength. Small pond, big fish. Then find bigger ponds.
